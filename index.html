The Games

Here are a bunch of games to help you to practise your guitar theory. There are three skills we strive to develop, and within each skill are three types of games. The skills are 1) Knowing the notes on your fretboard, 2) Knowing the intevals on your fretboard, 3) Knowing chords on your fretboard. Within each skill, the 3 games are 1) Identify: Seeing it played on the guitar, and being able to recognise what is being played. 2) Play: Can you play the notes we give you? 3) Read: given a score, can you read the note, and maybe play it too!

Single Notes

Identify Single Notes

How to play: Either: picture of a guitar will appear. Which note is circled? To score high, be fast, and accurate!
Or: we will give you a note and click the right note of the fretboard to get the point.

Play Single Notes

How to play: We will give you a note. Play the note on your guitar (make sure it is in tune!). Your mic will pic up the sound and tell you if you're correct!

Read Single Notes

How to play: A snippet of a score will appear. Select the correct note. To score high, be fast, and accurate!

Intervals

Identify Intervals

How to play: Either: A picture of a guitar will appear. Which interval is circled? To score high, be fast, and accurate!
Or: We will give you a interval and click the right notes of the fretboard to get the point.

Play Intervals

How to play: We will give you an inteval. Play the interval one note after the other on your guitar (make sure it is in tune!). Your mic will pic up the sound and tell you if you're correct!

Read Intervals

How to play: A snippet of a score will appear. Select the correct interval. To score high, be fast, and accurate!

Harmony

Identify Chords

How to play: Either picture of a guitar will appear. Which chord is circled? To score high, be fast, and accurate!
Or we will give you a chord and click the right fretboard diagram.

Play Chords

How to play: We will give you a chord. Play the chord on your guitar (make sure it is in tune!). Check if you are correct yourself by matching it with one of the fretboard diagrams displayed.

Read Chords

How to play: A snippet of a score will appear. Select the correct chord. To score high, be fast, and accurate!

Mixup

This is a mixup of all the challenges above!
